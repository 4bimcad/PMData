Payment Process

1. Contact Owner Information Report ($29):
If you request the contact details of a concession owner, you simply pay $29 and you will shortly receive your personalized PDF report directly by email.

2. Data Update or Project Analysis:
If you need to update information,add your concession project for investor or add analysis to your project, please send us the required file or a request by email. After completing the payment, our team will update the data and attach the new files to your project.

3. Advanced Services:
For services such as Due Diligence Report for Investors or Project Package for Concession Owners, we sign a contract with you. A 100% upfront payment is required, and all terms and details are clearly defined in the agreement. Work is carried out individually for each client.
